AgroGuard

Multimodal Approach for Plant Disease Detection Using YOLO and DenseNet Models

My project leverages advances in machine learning to introduce a novel multimodal approach that combines YOLO and DenseNet models. This combination not only improves the accuracy of disease identification, but also speeds up the detection process. Specifically, YOLO's powerful target detection capabilities are used to pinpoint disease symptoms on plant leaves, while DenseNet makes a more detailed and nuanced classification of these diseases. By addressing the challenge of timely and accurate disease detection, this project aims to provide farmers with a powerful solution that promises to transform plant disease management and crop maintenance strategies. The application of such advanced technology in agriculture promises to improve crop management and reduce crop losses due to disease.
